Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how RekietaLabs (a personal/community project, not a registered business) collects and uses your information when you interact with our websites, tools, and services.

1. What We Collect

  • Basic Usage Info: IP address, browser type, general location (city/state), and device info.
  • Account Data: If you create an account in one of our services, we may collect a UUID or optional display name.
  • Voluntary Info: Any info you give us through forms, contact pages, or feedback.

2. What We Use It For

  • To provide you access to RekietaLabs tools and platforms
  • To improve performance and fix bugs
  • To keep the experience safe and fair for all users
  • To respond if you reach out to us for help

3. What We Don't Do

  • We don’t sell your data
  • We don’t run ads or trackers
  • We don’t ask for unnecessary personal details

4. Sharing of Information

We don’t share your info unless:

  • It’s needed to operate our services (e.g., cloud storage or authentication tools)
  • We’re legally required to (rare, but possible)

5. Data Security

We use basic digital protections (such as secure connections and limited access) to protect your data. However, since RekietaLabs is not a corporation, please understand we cannot offer enterprise-grade security.

6. Your Rights

  • You can request that we delete your data
  • You can ask what data we have about you

Email us at support@rekietalabs.com to request anything.

7. Children’s Privacy

RekietaLabs is not intended for users under 13. If you are under 13, please don’t use our services.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We’ll update the “Last Updated” date when we do.
