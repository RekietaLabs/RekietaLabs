Privacy Policy for RekietaLabs Bot

Last Updated: 3/18/25 [Date]

1. Introduction

Welcome to RekietaLabs Bot! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect any information obtained through our Discord bot.

2. Information We Collect

  • User IDs & Server IDs (for bot functionality).
  • Messages & Commands (only for responding to commands, no message logging).
  • Channel IDs (if required for bot features).
  • Direct Messages (only if bot commands require them).

3. How We Use Your Information

We only use data for the following:

  • Processing bot commands and responses.
  • Storing server preferences.
  • Improving bot functionality.
  • Maintaining moderation logs (if applicable).

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data.

4. Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data. However, as the bot operates within Discord’s infrastructure, Discord’s security measures also apply.

5. User Rights & Data Removal

If you wish to remove your data, you can:

  • Use a bot command (if available) to clear stored data.
  • Contact us at [support@rekietalabs.com].

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions, contact us at [support@rekietalabs.com] or join our Support Server.
