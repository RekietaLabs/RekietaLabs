Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2025

Welcome to MyLabs Accounts by RekietaLabs. This is a personal/community project and not a registered business. We care about your privacy and want to explain how we handle your information when you use our services.

1. What We Collect

We collect a minimal amount of data to help MyLabs work properly:

  • Account ID: A UUID (randomly generated unique ID) used to identify your account.
  • Basic Metadata: Information like your device type, browser, and general region (from IP address) to improve performance and security.
  • Optional Data: If you enter a display name or other profile info, that data is stored too.

2. How We Use Your Info

We use your information only to:

  • Create and manage your MyLabs account
  • Display your progress or saved data
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Keep the platform secure and fair

3. What We Don’t Do

  • We don’t sell your data
  • We don’t run ads or trackers
  • We don’t collect more than we need

4. Sharing Your Info

We don’t share your personal info with others unless:

  • It’s needed to run the platform (e.g., Appwrite database hosting)
  • We’re legally required to (unlikely, but possible)

5. Your Choices

You can request to:

  • Delete your account
  • Access or update any optional info you’ve shared

Just email us at support@rekietalabs.com.

6. Data Security

We use basic security tools (like secure hosting and authentication) to protect your information. But please understand: this is a small-scale project, and no system is perfect.

7. For Younger Users

MyLabs is not meant for users under 13. We don’t knowingly collect data from children. If you’re under 13, please don’t use MyLabs.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy if we change how the platform works. We’ll update the "Last updated" date above when that happens.
